Magis

Web design

A website that offers a broad variety of furniture designed by the most famous designers around the world.

Aims of the project:

In this particular project we were focusing on the users navigation thru the website in order to get a “fast, easy and intuitive” process.
Giving the users information about the products and the company and to give them feel full control over the website.

Expected outcome:

To build a strong relationship between Magis and the user based on trust and accessibility.

Who’s going to benefit from it:

Different types of users such as:
Architects, interior designers, organizations and common users.
